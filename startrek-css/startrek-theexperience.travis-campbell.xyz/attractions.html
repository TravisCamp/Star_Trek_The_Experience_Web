History of the Future

The world's only Star Trek museum with over 200 permanent items from original costumes to props and prestige no longer made memorabilia. Prepare to see scripts from Star Trek : The Original Series, outfits worn by the cast in The Next Generation, and many of the props used in the movies and shows alike.

Guided Tour available for $10 / person.
