Moogie's Trading Post

A trading post ran by none other than Moogie, the ferengi that brought about a revolutionary change to their society. Here you can find collectibles and memorabilia of the Star Trek franchise that can't be found anywhere else. Along with Star Trek memorabilia, you can find items about space itself to give to friends.

Garak's Clothiers

Whether you need a shirt to remind you of your Federation officer days, or a hat saying you've been to the Alpha Quadrant, Garak's Clothiers can provide apparel to suit your every need. Ran by none other than Star Trek's favorite cardassian, Garak, you can find countless secrets hidden among the clothes sold here. Find shirts and outfits specially tailored for people of this century along with accessories to go along with any outfit you can think of.

Latinum Jewelers

Looking for premium jewelry that is coveted by Ferengi all across the galaxy? Look no further than Latinum Jewelers who seek to satisfy any and all request for Star Trek themed jewelry. Although they specialize in jewelry and trinkets primarily, they also do sell more exclusive and limited apparel that are likely to run out of stock.
