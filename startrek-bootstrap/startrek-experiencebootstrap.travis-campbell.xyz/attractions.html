Live Action Shows

Klingon Encounter

Experience a battle that will never be forgotten in song and story, for fighting Klingons is no easy task. Embark on a journey to prevent the disastrous plan that a group of rogue Klingons have came up with to destroy Captain Picard and the USS Enterprise. Perhaps one of you will be the hero the Federation needs to save one of its greatest captains.

Performance Length: ~18 minutes.

Borg Invasion 4D

With the combination of 3D and 4D effects, prepare to fight for your life while trying to survive the incursion from the most ruthless enemies the Federation has ever battled, the Borg. Using tactile experience, clever plans, and the tools at your disposal, prevent the Borg from assimilating the crew or else the Federation may lose a valuable foothold in the sector. Will you be the next to be assimilated?

Performance Length: ~18 minutes.

Museum

History of the Future

The world's only Star Trek museum with over 200 permanent items from original costumes to props and prestige no longer made memorabilia. Prepare to see scripts from Star Trek : The Original Series, outfits worn by the cast in The Next Generation, and many of the props used in the movies and shows alike.

Guided Tour available for $10 / person.
