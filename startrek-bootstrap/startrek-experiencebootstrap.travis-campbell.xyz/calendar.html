Featured Events

Lieutenant Worf Arriving for Duty

Lieutenant Worf, the sole klingon who serves the Federation of Planets, has been drawn into the spacial anomaly that brought the establishment to the 21st century. Until the spacial anomaly occurs again, and the shuttlecraft repaired, expect Worf to wander the base and watch out for his Bat'leth as some of the other Klingon warriors may provoke a fight!

October 4, 2021 - October 13, 2021

Become a Federation Officer Today

Ever since the war with the Dominion, Starfleet has been seeking officers to assist in bringing back a better tomorrow for the Federation an it's citizens. At the President's bequest, Admiral Ross has entered the station to seek potential officers. Seek him out in the Captain's Lodge to inquire about applications.

October 10, 2021 - October 16, 2021

Liquidator Brunt's Acquisition

Liquidator Brunt has siezed Moogie's Trading Post. Fortunately, the Grand Nagus has stepped in to prevent her shop from being completely taken. Unfortunately for Moogie, Brunt has lowered prices to push her out of the station. Fortunately for the Federation, prices have never been better for memorabilia of other planets..

October 1, 2021 - October 20, 2021

Future Events

Memory Scavenger Hunt

Doctor Bashir gets attacked by a Lethean once again. Help him recover his memories within the Prominade by gathering his personal belongings. Whoever helps him the most will get a special means of thanks.

October 25, 2021 - November 1, 2021

William Riker commands the station

While Major Kira is away on Bajor, Commander William Riker has been instructed to command the station. During this time, he will wander the promenade and interact with the crew.

December 13, 2021 - December 16, 2021
