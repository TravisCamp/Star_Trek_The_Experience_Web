Our Mission

We here at Star Trek : The Experience aim to provide an environment where fans of Star Trek can walk among the notorious characters they have grown to love. These characters have served as idols for some, and brought about changes into the industry by showing diversity under the guise of a futuristic society. Along with the depictions of the characters, we aim to create an environment where all can enjoy themselves and experience a place where everyone is welcomed. With these depictions we wish to pay homage to the incredible changes to the entertainment industry that Star Trek helped bring to reality.

We hope to create that same impact on fans. Here they can meet others who look up to the same characters, interact with their childhood heroes, and have hopes for a brighter tomorrow.

Support Us

You can join us as one of the actors who portray the numerous characters walking around the station, or perhaps help do the makeup and design the cosmetics required for the costumes of our beloved characters.

If you want to support us in a different way, you can also donate to help fund future exhibits and live action performances to better display the Star Trek universe.
